The GCC Journey

From first question to full scale.

Setting up a GCC in India is one of the best decisions a global company can make. But the details matter. We have seen what works and what quietly drains value.

1

Coaching Before You Commit

Before you invest, we help you ask the right questions about location, structure, talent, compliance, and long-term operating model.

2

Advantages of Building in India

Deep talent pools, lower operating costs, strong regulatory clarity, and a growing ecosystem of innovation. We help you build the right business case internally.

3

Milestones and Pitfalls

Most GCC delays happen at entity setup, leadership hiring, and governance handover. We help you see them coming and move past them faster.

4

Hands-On Support

We do not only advise. We work alongside your team through setup, first hires, finance operations, compliance, and the first 100 days of operations.

From GCC to GIC — The Bigger Opportunity

Once your GCC is stable, the bigger opportunity is to transform it into a Global Innovation Center that drives product thinking, tech leadership, and strategic value back to the parent organization. We help you make that shift with the right structure, execution discipline, and long-term business thinking.
