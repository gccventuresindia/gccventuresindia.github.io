The Conglomerate Problem — Solved

⚠️ What Happens Without Safeguards

  • Scenario 1: You build a venture for 3 years with Conglomerate funding. They reject the exit — keeping all your growth work, paying you nothing.
  • Scenario 2: They incubate the venture using your effort, then buy it internally at a low valuation — bypassing your advisory fee entirely.
  • Result: 3 years of effort, zero upside. No legal recourse without pre-signed protections.

✅ How We Protect You

  • Retainer + Success Fee split — you earn monthly regardless of exit outcome.
  • Non-Circumvention Agreement — Conglo cannot buy directly without paying your fee.
  • Exit Veto Clause — rejection without cause triggers 100% fee payment.
  • IP Ownership — all models, decks, and strategy documents are yours until paid.
  • Milestone Gates — 6-month Go/No-Go reviews to exit engagement early if needed.
