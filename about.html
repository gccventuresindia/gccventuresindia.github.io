About

Why GCC Ventures India exists

GCC Ventures India supports large global companies that want more than a standard captive center. The goal is to build a business-owned innovation house in India with clear governance, the right operating model, and strong execution discipline.

Our thinking

The model is BOT-led, so the global conglomerate owns the entity from day one while the center is built and stabilized with hands-on support.

Alongside innovation center setup, the firm also supports M&A advisory, venture exit, and executive coaching for experienced domain experts.

  • Focused on long-term capability building.
  • Practical, operations-grounded advisory.

Who we serve
