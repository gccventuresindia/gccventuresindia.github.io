Our Story

How we started.

Sanjeev spent 20+ years building and scaling Global Capability Centers across Asia. Nagaraj built ventures and exited. Both saw the same problem: founders and leaders understood their business, but they were navigating unfamiliar territory—GCC complexity, India dynamics, exit strategy—without reliable guidance.

So we started GCC Ventures India. Not as consultants with templated recommendations. As coaches and advisors who've lived this. We work with companies on the things that matter: clarity on strategy, confidence in execution, and conviction on timing.

That's it. Three angles. One platform.
