Questions

What's the difference between building as a GCC vs traditional startup structure? +

GCC structure gives you operating cost efficiency, talent access, and a clear path to scale. It's ideal if you're part of a larger organization or planning significant India investment. Traditional startup structure is leaner but requires you to build everything yourself. We help you pick the right one for your situation.

How long does it take to get a venture launch-ready? +

3–6 months depending on your starting point. If you have a founding team and capital, that's fast. If you need everything designed from scratch, it takes longer. We work at your pace and don't hold you back.

Do you help with fundraising strategy? +

Yes, but that's not our primary focus. What we do is make sure your operating model and governance are investor-grade. We help you articulate that clearly in fundraising conversations.

What if we're already operational and just need a governance audit? +

We do that. We can audit your current structure, identify gaps, and help you fix them. It's faster than a full redesign and often all you need.

Can you help with GBS setup specifically? +

Yes. Sanjeev's expertise is in GBS and shared services. Finance operations, O2C, P2P processes, automation. That's a natural part of venture build-out if you're thinking global from day one.
